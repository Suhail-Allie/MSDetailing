~ OUR STORY ~

MS Detailing & Carwash was established in 2019 in Cape Town, South Africa, born out of a passion for cars and a commitment to providing top-notch auto detailing services. Over the years, MS Detailing has built a reputation for meticulous attention to detail and a customer-first approach, offering a range of services from thorough hand washes to advanced paint correction and ceramic coatings. With a focus on quality and professionalism, MS Detailing & Carwash has become a trusted name among Cape Town’s car enthusiasts, ensuring vehicles are treated with the care they deserve.
